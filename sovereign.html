Sovereign

Official harbour stamp branding.

One of the largest yachts available for charter in Australia, Sovereign proudly offers European standards to an emerging region. At 44.2m, SOVEREIGN has been recently launched to the charter market by her experienced Owners.

SOVEREIGN required a new official harbour stamp for all purchasing orders and international travel requirements. The brief required a traditional nautical theme with information simple and easy to read.
