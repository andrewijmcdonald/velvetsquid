Subscription Box Service

Velvet Squid was contracted by Babyology to design and build a landing page for their new subscription boxes. The project had a very short delivery schedule and required some seriously fast design and development. The landing page integrated an e-Commerce solution that allowed visitors to purchase a variety of packages.

Porject Breakdown.

The challenge

Working to an insanely tight deadline and a client with a highly established reputation for quality.

Key components

Design.

The design needed to reflect the clients existing style guide which included the use of fonts and colour palettes. The solution needed a clean, simple, and striking design.

Concept.

The concept came together quite quickly with the objective to keep everything accessible on one page. Scrolling from top to bottom, the user would be pitched the product, it's benefits, read testimonials, and be directed to call-to-action purchasing buttons.

Delivery.

The final deliveries included a complete HTML website, integrated into a third-party subscription package tool to track orders and manage custom service.
