cgstudentawards.com

Creative community for young digital artists.

Client:CG Student Awards Pty Ltd.

Date:12 January, 2015

Link:www.cgstudentawards.com

Type:Redesign, Branding, Website, Video

The CG Student Awards is a creative community for young digital artists studying Visual Effects, Animation, and Next-Gen Gaming. The backbone of the website includes a portfolio system for students, and a review system for the judging panel.

The client required a complete redesign of their existing site, brand identity, and an overhaul of the backend systems in place to deal with 500,000 monthly visitors and over 1,000 student entries submitted over a 3 month peak period.
