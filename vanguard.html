Vanguard Investments

Key Facts Statement iPad App.

Client:Vangaurd Investements Australia Ltd.

Date:01 February, 2015

Link:https://www.vanguardinvestments.com.au/

Type:Wireframing, Mockups, iPad Application

Short description.

The challenge

Working with a high profile corporate client with a strong legacy of tradition was a challenging experience. The goal was to push them beyond their comfort zone and convert a content heavy document into an engaging iPad Application.

Short description.

The process

After a string of innitial meetings, our team spent a huge amount of time developing wireframe mockups and concepts. These were used to test the theory and user experience. Once approved, the team built a full working prototype.

Short description.

The solution

The solution was simple. Condense a content heavy key facts statement into a beautiful and simple digital brochure. Gamification was introduced, bright colours, and subtle animations were all used to enhance the experience.
