Diamond Care Dental

This month the creative team has selected a dental website from Sydney.

Team review and scores.

First Impressions

The user experience was outdated, a little confusing, and didn't reflect the same quality of service as presented at the surgery.

  • Design

    Score42%

    Typical Layout and Design

    The design is very typical of a dentist website. Blue and white colour theme, standard menu items. Lots of text and stock photos.

  • Branding

    Score54%

    Safe and Suitable

    The logo design and general branding of the business is actually quite suitable. The logo can work at both small and large scale. The name is clear, and the tooth design works.

  • User Experience

    Score38%

    Confusing and Cluttered

    Customers have a goal when they find a website. They want to find out key information, determine if the service can be trusted, learn about the people behing the business, and make contact quickly and easily. This website attempts these goals, but makes a number of basic mistakes that hinders this goal.

  • Technology

    Score64%

    Wordpress and Apache

    The website runs on Wordpress which is an excellent solution for this business. However, the server configurement and lack of a cloud-based server is a concern. The speed is also a major concern.

  • Social

    Score32%

    Facebook only

    The Facebook page is a good decision for this company, however they are lacking a newsletter which is the most successful approach to contacting customers to offer deals and provide updates.
